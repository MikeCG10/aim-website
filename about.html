About AIM

A CSUSB student organization affiliated with the Center for Cyber & AI — built on AI, InfoSec, and mentorship.

Our mission

AIM was established to provide CSUSB students with mentorship, guidance, and learning opportunities in cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and information security. We help students understand and apply cybersecurity frameworks used in the field — including the NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0, NIST SP 800-53, and other relevant government, industry, and security frameworks — while developing technical skills and preparing for national cybersecurity competitions.

What we focus on

Artificial Intelligence

From foundational ML concepts to building and fine-tuning LLM-powered applications, our AI track pairs workshops with team projects members can put on a resume.

Information Security

Applied training in cybersecurity frameworks, ethical hacking labs, and competition preparation for events across the CAE Cyber Competition Atlas Map.

Mentorship

Mentee members are matched with Mentor members across technical, competition, framework, career, research, or general focus areas.

Frameworks & competitions

We build our curriculum and training around the standards and competitions professionals actually use.

NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 NIST SP 800-53
IE Mayor's Cyber Cup National Cyber League (NCL) NCAE Cyber Games CCDC CPTC

Affiliation

AIM is affiliated with the CSUSB Center for Cyber & AI, located at California State University, San Bernardino, 5500 University Parkway, San Bernardino, CA 92407, Office JB-282.
