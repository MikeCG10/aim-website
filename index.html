Three pillars, one club

Everything we do falls under one of these focus areas.

AI

Workshops and projects on machine learning, LLMs, and applied AI tools, from first model to research-grade builds.

InfoSec

Hands-on cybersecurity built around real frameworks like NIST CSF 2.0 and SP 800-53, plus national competition prep.

Mentorship

Every member can be matched with a mentor: technical, competition, framework, career, research, or general guidance.

Built for competition

Members train for national and regional cybersecurity competitions, including:

IE Mayor's Cyber Cup National Cyber League (NCL) NCAE Cyber Games National Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC) Global Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition (CPTC)
